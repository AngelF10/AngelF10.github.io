Cake with Cherries Recipe (Торtа со вишни)

History

This is a traditional dish that is made in Europe. This dish is also a part of the Macedonian cuisine and is an imporant part in our culture. People usually make this dish for a tradition but we also make it when we want to. We either make it for guests or for ourselves. This dish is really delicous. And this dish is brought down from generations to generations. It's the taste of our culture.

This recipe makes the cake and it guides you step by step on how to make it. If you need help, there is a video that is attached that shows how it is made. Have fun cooking and eating this delicious dish.

Ingredients

  • 5 eggs
  • 300g, sugar
  • 200ml, yougurt
  • 170ml, oil
  • 1/2 bag of baking powder
  • 270g, flour
  • 1 vanilla powder
  • 400g, cherries
  • Powdered sugar

Directions

  1. Take the seeds out of the cherries and drain the juice out of them.
  2. Separate the yolks and whites.
  3. Using a mixer, mix the eggs white while slowly adding sugar. And add the podwer of vanilla inside the bowl.
  4. Then add the egg yolk mixture to the egg whites and carefully mix with the whisk.
  5. Next add the yogurt and oil and mix the whisk again.
  6. Finally,add the flour in the bowl that you mixed the pastry in. Mix again with the whisk and pour the pastry into a 35x20 baking sheet, you should either grease this with oil and flour, or put baking paper on it.
  7. Arrange the cherries on top of the mixture, press them lightly.
  8. Put in to bake in a preheated over at 200 degrees for the first ten minutes and then reduce it to 180 degrees until it's fully baked. You'll know when it's baked by the cake darkening easily.
  9. Once you take it out of the oven, sprinkle it lightly with powdered sugar and it's ready to be eaten.
