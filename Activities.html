Taekwondo

I started taekwondo at the start of 7th grade. I have always wanted to do martial arts and now I felt ready and I wanted to become disciplined. So far, I've competed one time and I got first and second places which was pretty good. I practice a lot for taekwondo and I strive to improve my flexibility and balance every day.

Crochet Club

Crochet club is so fun. I have a few friends that do it with me so we just talk to each other while we crochet. The people there offer yarn for students that don't have some and we can literally just chill and crochet which is super fun.

Volleyball Club

Volleyball club is awesome. I've been to two meetings so far and it was great. We don't have it in the winter since it's cold outside. I have a few friends who are in it and I hope we can start playing volleyball again soon.
